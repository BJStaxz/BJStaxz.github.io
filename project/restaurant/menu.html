Lightly Cured Kampachi

$27.95

A "Lightly Cured Kampachi" refers to a slice of raw Kampachi (also known as Hawaiian Yellowtail) fish that has been gently cured in a mixture of salt, sugar, and sometimes citrus, resulting in a subtly enhanced flavor with a slightly firmer texture, while still retaining its rich, buttery fat content and delicate taste, perfect for serving as crudo or in a sashimi dish; the "light" cure means the fish is not heavily seasoned, allowing its natural flavor to shine through.
