Five Salmon Marinades


         
Total Time: 1 hour         Make 1: Serving             Author: Benjamin Brown


INGREDIENTS
TERIYAKI SALMON
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 2 tablespoons light brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon chopped green onions
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds
GREEK LEMON & HERB
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon fresh dill
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • /2 to 1 teaspoon salt, adjust to taste
  • 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, to taste
MAPLE MUSTARD
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 3 tablespoons dijon or yellow mustard
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/2 to 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
CHIPOTLE ADOBO
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons adobo chipotle paste
  • 1 teaspoon fresh garlic
  • 1/2 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1/2 to 1 teaspoon salt, to taste
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

    • HOT HONEY HARISSA
     
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 2 tablespoons harissa
    • 1/2 tablespoon garlic, finely minced
    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    • 1/2 tablespoon parsley, finely chopped
    • 1/2 tablespoon cilantro, finely chopped
    • 1 teaspoon paprika
    • 1/2 teaspoon coriander
    • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
    • 1/2 to 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1 to 2 tablespoons honey, to taste

    INSTRUCTIONS

    TERIYAKI SALMON
    1. In a small bowl, mix ginger paste, garlic paste, light brown sugar, soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, and black pepper .

    2. Stir in chopped green onions and sesame seeds.

    3. Add two salmon filets into a Ziploc bag, pour the marinade over,seal, and then freeze.
      4. GREEK LEMON & HERB

    4. In a bowl, whisk together olive oil, fresh dill, lemon juice,minced garlic, fresh thyme, and dried oregano.

    5. Add Dijon mustard, salt, and black pepper, adjusting the seasonings to taste.

    6. Add two salmon filets into a Ziploc bag, pour the marinade over, seal, and then freeze.
      7. MAPLE MUSTARD
    7. Combine maple syrup, Dijon or yellow mustard,Worcestershire sauce, and minced garlic in a bowl.

    8. Season with salt and black pepper, adjusting to your preference.

    9. Add two salmon filets into a Ziploc bag, pour the marinade over,seal, and then freeze.
      10. CHIPOTLE ADOBO
    10. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, adobo chipotle paste, fresh garlic, and chili powder.

    11. Add salt, coriander, oregano, cumin, and black pepper.

    12. Add two salmon filets into a Ziploc bag, pour the marinade over,seal, and then freeze.

    13. Combine olive oil, harissa, finely minced garlic, and lemon juice in a bowl.
      14. HOT HONEY HARISSA
    14. Stir in parsley, cilantro, paprika, coriander, cumin, and salt.

    15. Add honey to taste and mix until smooth.

    16. Add two salmon filets into a Ziploc bag, pour the marinade over, seal, and then freeze.

             

    BBQ Salmon Taco


    Total Time: 30 Min         Make 1: Serving             Author: Benjamin Brown


    INGREDIENTS

    • 1 pound salmon, skin removed and cubed
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1 teaspoon salt, adjust to taste
    • 1 teaspoon chili powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
    • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
    • 1/2 teaspoon coriander
    • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
    • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    • 1/4 cup BBQ sauce, more to taste
    • oil spray

    INSTRUCTIONS

    1. To a bowl, add the cubed salmon along with the olive oil, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, chili powder, paprika, cumin, coriander, oregano, and black pepper. Mix to combine evenly.

    2. Preheat air fryer to 400Â°F.

    3. Spray the tray with oil to prevent sticking. Add the salmon cubes, making sure they're spaced apart.

    4. Air fry for 7 minutes.

    5. Open the air fryer and brush with the barbecue sauce.

    6. Air fry for 1 to 2 minutes more.

    7. Take out of the air fryer.

    8. To a tortilla, add the avocado, lettuce or cabbage, sour cream, salmon, corn, cotija, and cilantro to enjoy!

      9. Crab Rangoon Toast



      Total Time: 45 Min         Make 1: Serving             Author: Benjamin Brown


      INGREDIENTS

      • 1 pound crab imitation meat
      • 1/2 cup kewpie mayo
      • tablespoon low-sodium soy sauce
      • 1 egg white
      • 1/3 cup cream cheese, room temp
      • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
      • 1/2 teaspoon salt
      • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
      • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped
      • 3 tablespoons cornstarch

      INSTRUCTIONS

      1. In a large bowl, add the imitation crab meat, Kewpie mayo, soy sauce, egg white, and cream cheese. Mix until everything is well combined.

      2. Add the garlic powder, salt, black pepper, and chopped green onions. Stir to incorporate.

      3. Sprinkle in the cornstarch and gently fold it into the mixture to help bind the filling together. Set the crab filling aside.

      4. Take the stale white bread slices and trim off the crusts if preferred. You will need pairs of slices for each sandwich.

      5. You can leave the bread as whole slices or cut each slice in half for smaller sandwiches, depending on your preference.

      6. Spread a generous amount of crab filling on one slice of bread in each pair. Top with a second slice of bread to create a sandwich.

      7. Press down lightly on the sandwich to ensure the filling adheres well.

      8. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet or deep pan over medium heat, ensuring the oil is deep enough for shallow frying (about 1/4 to 1/2 inch).

      9. Once the oil is hot, carefully place the sandwiches in the pan. Fry for 2-3 minutes until the bottom is golden and crispy.

      10. Flip the sandwiches and fry the other side for another 2-3 minutes until itâs golden and crispy.

      11. Remove the sandwiches from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.

      12. Serve immediately with your favorite dipping sauces, such as sweet chili or soy sauce.
